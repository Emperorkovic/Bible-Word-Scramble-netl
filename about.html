Growing in faith, one word at a time

Our Story

Bible Word Scramble was born from a simple idea: what if learning Scripture could feel like play? We believe that when we engage our minds in playful ways, we open our hearts to deeper understanding of God's Word.

Our team of believers, educators, and developers came together to create a game that is fun for the whole family — children learning their first Bible names, teenagers quizzing each other on scripture, and adults revisiting beloved stories from across both Testaments.

Every word in our database has been carefully chosen to reflect the richness of Scripture — from familiar names like Noah and Mary, to deeper words like Sanctification and Predestination that invite us to explore theology.

Our Mission

We want to make the Bible accessible and enjoyable for everyone. Whether you're a lifelong churchgoer or someone just beginning to explore the Christian faith, Bible Word Scramble meets you where you are.

We believe in:

  • Making scripture engagement fun and habitual
  • Building a free resource available to anyone, anywhere
  • Honouring the Word of God with reverence and joy
  • Creating a welcoming space for people of all denominations

The Game

Bible Word Scramble features over 1000 scripture-inspired words across three difficulty levels — Easy, Medium, and Hard — and five categories including Old Testament, New Testament, Biblical People, and Biblical Places. New words and challenges are added regularly.

The game works offline once installed and can be added to your phone's home screen for quick access. It's designed to be a calming, joyful daily ritual — a few minutes of scripture-inspired play to start or end your day.

A Note on Faith

Bible Word Scramble is a non-denominational resource. We respect and welcome Christians from all traditions — Catholic, Protestant, Penticost, Orthodox, and everything in between. Our word selections draw from across the canon of Scripture as it is broadly understood.

We are not a replacement for your church, your pastor, or your personal Bible study. We're a small, fun supplement — a little treat for the scripturally-curious mind. 🙏
